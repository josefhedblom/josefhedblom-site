Create Random Playlist

Sometimes, you just want to listen to an artist or a band on your way to work, school, or a match. You want some songs quickly and easily without having to think about it. I personally use Apple Music, and opening the app, navigating to either your library or the search bar, and then starting to click around is not what I want. I just want a playlist that generates itself quickly and easily. The solution: create a shortcut that does it for me. You run the shortcut, and a prompt appears. You type in the artist or band, and a playlist with 15 random songs is created. Two simple steps: open, type, and then listen! Download and try it yourself.
